Our Services

Food Orders

Gourmet Dining, Delivered to Your Door: Indulge in an exquisite culinary experience from the comfort of your room with our 24/7 in-room dining service. Whether you're craving a late-night snack, a hearty breakfast, or a multi-course dinner, our diverse menu offers a selection of gourmet dishes crafted by our world-class chefs. From international delicacies to local specialties, every meal is prepared with the freshest ingredients and presented with elegance. Enjoy fine dining at your convenience, any time of day or night.

Housekeeping Requests

At Hotel Paradise, we take pride in maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and comfort. Our housekeeping team is dedicated to ensuring your room is always in pristine condition. Daily cleaning services include meticulous attention to detail, from fresh linens and towels to perfectly arranged amenities. Should you require any additional services, such as turndown service or extra toiletries, our staff is just a call away. Experience a spotless, serene environment, where every detail is thoughtfully managed for your comfort and relaxation.
